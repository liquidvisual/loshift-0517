Receive quotations for transport services by posting your request for quotation.

It's as easy as:

OR

Once your quote request has been posted, It is added to the "Loadboard" and then alerted to the loadshift community.

Thoes carriers that may be of assistance respond directly to you with quotation (Varing price & Avaliability).

Loadshift

The Place Where Transport Does Business!

Note: loadshift.com.au is an online business service that specialises in the heavy load transport industry. It simply delivers your quotation request into transport businesses. Loadshift DOES NOT participate in any quotation, nor does it receive commissions. You deal directly with the transport carriers at all times.

Carriers subscribe to this marketplace (on a monthly basis), to receive your quote request as a job lead into their businesses.
